Untying the Knots of your Life.

Think about you current problem... this problem is a great big knot, and all you can see is the knot. Well lets turn it side ways, there is a rope coming out of this knot with other knots along the rope.

These knots on the rope are seeds of the present problem, they are situations/experiences we have had which have contributed to the current problem. The brain's perception was formed with the first knot, where it decided that this type of experience was harmful so any other situation similar to the first one, the brain reacted to protect us and created another knot.

By using your energetic database/ the aura, life retuning finds each and every knot relevant to the current problem and unties them. When we look back at the big knot/the current problem, it is no longer there as the perception of the brain has changed. Once the perception of the problem has changed, the usual result is the change will be permanent.
