Life Retuning

What is Life Retuning?

Life Retuning is a total treatment system.

It works on every level of consciousness, using the human energy field.

No matter whether you are feeling fear, anxiety, cravings, low moods or any other distressing emotions. Life Retuning tracks and deals with the root cause of the problem.

Life Retuning reads the human energy field as a data base, it does not miss out on any information that is relevant to a problem. The energy fields reveals all and also indicates when a treatment is fully completed.

What does a Life Retuning treatment involve?

A Life Retuning session starts with the present problem being identified by the client, I keep questioning about the problem to a minimum because Life Retuning is not a talking therapy.

Through muscle testing, I will identify ‘seed traumas’ linked to the current problem and will then ask you to tap a specific sequence of points.

I will make sure that all aspects of the problem which are ready to be treated are dealt with and that your energy system is balanced and in harmony.

Through this treatment we untie the knots of perception our brain has put in place and once the perception of a problem has changed, the usual result is that the change will be permanent.

Life Retuning works successfully with the following problems.

Anxiety
Phobias and fears
Love pain and relationship break-up pain
Cravings, addictions and OCD
Low moods and low energy
Trauma, from any past life experience and PTSD
Feelings of guilt, jealousy, shame and embarrassment
Grief and bereavement
Low self-confidence, low self-esteem and low self-worth
Intimidation, bullying and vulnerability to other people
Anger, rage and frustration
and any other distressing emotions or thoughts.
