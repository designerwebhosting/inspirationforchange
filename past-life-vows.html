Past Life Vows

There are many reasons for why we may take a vow in a past life, it could be a religious vow, a vow of allegiance, or any other time when you had a powerful intention to belong to a particular group or person. These patterns affect many of the decisions you make in this lifetime.

You may be carrying the energy of past life vows and contracts, pacts or judgements. When you take a vow, whether it is for chastity, poverty, abstinence or any other reason, the energetic intention is strong and it is witnessed and so the energy created is very powerful. There are often no limits to these vows, meaning that not just your current life but also your future lives will be affected.

The same vow taken many times. We are likely to be holding more than one particular vow in our energy field. This happens because we are attracting a particular set of circumstances into our lives during each of our incarnations and therefore end up in a situation where we take another vow of the same type. The energy of these vows becomes an embedded command in our mind, affecting you now in your everyday life.

Using Life Retuning these redundant vows can be reversed and replaced with a new healthy energy pattern. You always need to balance up the energy of what you are letting go with a new energy of what we are attracting in.
